Intro to My Experience in the iSchool

Coming into college, I was not sure what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. All I knew was that I was prepared to try just about everything until I figured out. I was having a hard time finding classes that genuinely grasped my interest until I took Intro to Information Technology. I l found myself emersed in learning about today’s new technologies, how their applied, managed, and how they affect every aspect of modern life. This is what lead me to pursue a bachelor’s degree in Information Management and Technology. After enrolling in the iSchool, I was quick to learn that this major has so much more to offer than I could have ever imagined. That is why I have created this website, to share with all of you the various skillsets I have learned and experiences I have had.
